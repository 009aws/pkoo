About Us

Who We Are

For over 28 years, Infosoft Typing & Documentation Clearing has been a trusted name in the UAE, offering reliable documentation, business setup, and transaction services. Established in 1996, Infosoft has thrived with the support of satisfied local and foreign customers, as well as government agencies. With a skilled and passionate team, we deliver modern solutions while maintaining our premium standards.

Our range of services includes immigration, business setup, trademark registration, legal translation, and more. We are committed to professionalism, transparency, and punctuality. Our expert team ensures comprehensive and specialized services, and we provide free document pickup, online support, and flexible payment options.
